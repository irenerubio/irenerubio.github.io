About me

My name is Irene Rubio and I was born in Madrid (Spain) on the 5th October, in 1999. Right now, I'm studying a Higher Education Training Cycle of 3D Animation, Games and Interactive Media in the U-Tad university of Madrid. Even though I am currently studying a wide variety of subjects, I would like to focus in 3D animation and character modeling in the future. I hope that this two years I'll be studying I'll successfully finish my training and become someone in this new labor market. I hope you enjoy this portfolio!
